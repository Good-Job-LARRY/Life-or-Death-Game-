# Life-or-Death-Game-
You work for a hospital, and you've only been given one liver. But there are three patients on life support, and they all need that transplant to survive. As the doctor, you have to decide who deserves it the most. You'll make your choice based on different factors, like their net worth, their gender, or even just how they respond to a certain question. It’s all up to you. (All the answers to the questions and the individual patient details are randomized, so you can replay the game as many times as you want and get different scenarios every time.) Once you've chosen someone, just enter their ID then press enter on the keyboard.
